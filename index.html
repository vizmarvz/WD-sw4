ADIDAS SAMBA


Typically constructed with a mix of suede and leather, a contrasting gum sole and the three-stripe trademark. The Samba name in capitals is embossed in gold alongside the three stripes. During the 1990s, skateboarders started to wear Sambas.

ADIDAS CAMPUS


An athletic shoe created by Adidas that was released in the 1970s. The shoes were first released as basketball shoes before switching to lifestyle shoes. The unique design of the shoe helped make it popular as a chunkier alternative to Adidas' other sneakers.
