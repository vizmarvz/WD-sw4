CAMPUS KORN

is part of a collaboration between adidas and Korn, an American nu metal band. It pays homage to Korn's album "Follow The Leader," which was released in 1998. This special-edition sneaker boasts a low-top silhouette and a shaggy suede upper.

CAMPUS BAD BUNNY

Details. Simple yet timeless, the adidas Originals Bad Bunny Campus fuses nostalgia and modern style for versatile, everyday wear. Featuring a double-tongue and double-heel construction, these sneakers bring back the iconic Campus silhouette with a twist.

CAMPUS KORN 2.0

This Campus 2.0 rendition boasts a dark grey suede upper with clean white leather 3-Stripes covering the sidewalls. Black co-branded logos animate the leather heel tabs, while a stylish “Campus” print decorates the lateral quarter panel.

CAMPUS GREEN

A true classic, the adidas Campus 00s is back for the new season with an updated design, dressed in dark green with a contrasting white 3-Stripes logo to the sides. It is crafted from suede and sits atop a white rubber midsole - a nod to the sneaker heritage.

CAMPUS RED

These adidas shoes take the iconic elements of the Campus 80s and give them a next-gen, skateboarding-inspired twist. Known for its durability and the way it moulds to your foot over time, suede helps provide longevity and solid footing. New collegiate colourblocking, graphics and branding create a fresh identity for the next generation to own and style.

CAMPUS BLUE

An iconic silhouette that has influenced fashion, pop culture and beyond, the Campus returns with premium materials. These shoes flash an upper made of pigskin nubuck, giving them a street-ready look.

SYNTHETIC LEATHER USAGE

The synthetic material is known for its exceptional durability and resistance to various types of damage. Campus shoes made of these materials guarantee long-term use even in the most difficult conditions. Moreover, synthetics are highly waterproof, which is crucial for keeping feet dry during changeable weather conditions. Thanks to the use of advanced technologies, the shoes dry quickly and wick away moisture, which increases wearing comfort.

PIGSKIN NUBUCK LEATHER

Top Grain Leather, whose surface has been buffed and brushed to create a soft, velvety effect. Differs from suede in that while suede is created from the flesh (inner) side of a hide, nubuck is created using the grain (outer) side, giving it added strength and durability.
